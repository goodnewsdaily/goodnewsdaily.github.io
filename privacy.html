Privacy Policy

Data Collection

GoodNewsDaily does not collect personal data. We do not use cookies for tracking, advertising, or profiling.

Analytics

We use GoatCounter, a privacy-friendly, open-source analytics tool. GoatCounter does not use cookies, does not collect personal data, and is fully GDPR compliant. It only counts page views in aggregate.

Local Storage

We store your language preference in your browser's local storage (key: gnd-lang). This data never leaves your device and is only used to remember your chosen language.

Embedded Content

Some pages embed YouTube videos. When you play a video, YouTube's own privacy policy applies. We use the youtube.com/embed URL which loads YouTube's standard player.

Third-Party Services

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect technical data such as IP addresses for security purposes. See GitHub's Privacy Statement.
