About GoodNewsDaily

GoodNewsDaily is a free, ad-free platform dedicated to sharing positive stories, great music, and genuine humor from around the world.


We believe that good news deserves as much attention as bad news. The world is full of people solving problems, creating beauty, and helping each other — their stories deserve to be told.


No algorithms. No clickbait. No doom. Just good stuff, curated with care.

Our Values

☀️
Positivity First — We only share stories that inspire, inform, or make you smile.
🌍
Global Perspective — Good news happens everywhere, not just in English-speaking countries.
🔒
No Ads, No Tracking — Free forever, privacy-friendly, open to all.
✅
Real Stories — Everything we share is verified and sourced. No fake feel-good.
